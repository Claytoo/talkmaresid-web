StoryLine

PLOT

Little Nightmares begins the story on the lower floor of the Maw, a ship filled with grotesque and creepy humanoid monsters of various shapes and sizes. The main character of the game is a little girl in a yellow raincoat named Six, we will accompany her in the game as she tries to get out of the ship alive.

The game begins with Six having a nightmare in which a masked woman approaches her, after which she wakes up in a large suitcase. The girl, with the help of her lighter, enters the Prison for children, guarded by the long-armed Janitor. Gradually, she begins to experience attacks of hunger, which an unknown boy helps to satisfy Six by giving her piece of bread.

Soon the Janitor learns about the heroine’s escape, so he lures her with a piece of meat into a cage and carries her to the Lair. Having got out of it, Six attracts the attention of the monster and, thus, throughout the entire level he chases after it until it is driven into a room, blocking his path to the girl with a closing door. He sticks his hands into it to get Six, but she breaks the cage that held the door, cutting off the arm's of the Janitor.

Once in the the Kitchen, Six for the third time begins to starve. She had no choice but to eat a rat caught in mousetrap. On the way, she comes across twin chefs who immediately reacted aggressively to an uninvited guest. The heroine hardly manages to escape to the Guest Area with the help of hooks for carrying bundles of meat.

Soon the Janitor learns about the heroine’s escape, so he lures her with a piece of meat into a cage and carries her to the Lair. Having got out of it, Six attracts the attention of the monster and, thus, throughout the entire level he chases after it until it is driven into a room, blocking his path to the girl with a closing door. He sticks his hands into it to get Six, but she breaks the cage that held the door, cutting off the arm's of the Janitor.

In the next location, Six meets the Guests who have sailed to the Maw to eat dishes prepared specially for them. However, soon the guests went crazy to such an extent that they began to ferociously chase Six. There are more and more of them, and finally, the guests formed a wave, wanting to catch the heroine. They chase after her, but Six narrowly escapes from the wave of cannibals by swinging onto a lantern to a safe balcony. There, the girl begins to writhe again from hunger. a Nome, who stood in the way, decides to give her a sausage, but Six chooses to eat the unsuspecting creature instead.

Finally, once in the Lady's Quarters,
the heroine meets the masked woman who appeared in her nightmare.
Having taken the key to the door to the dressing room,
Six draws the attention of the Lady of the Maw,
which begins to chase after her, until the girl runs to the abandoned part of the palace. There, among the broken mirrors, she finds a small whole mirror, with the help of which the Six reflects the rays of light on the Lady, thereby weakening her. Feeling the fifth time hunger, Six bites into the neck of the lady.

Together with satisfying the hunger, the heroine gains the ability to absorb the souls of living beings, which she used at a party, thus getting out of the Maw to the surface..
