Key features

EMBARK ON A GRIM ADVENTURE

Confront your childhood fears in this darkly whimsical tale, set in an immersive world of great sensibility, interactive storytelling and outstanding sound design.

VISIT THE MOST INTRIGUING DOLLHOUSE

VISIT THE MOST INTRIGUING DOLLHOUSE

Each room is a cell, each resident a threat, and both are puzzles to untangle. Reconnect with your inner child, unleash your imagination, and stay out of harm's way..

character

six
a Hungry Child
Trapped in the darkest dephts of The Maw, starving and alone Six's world is one filled with danger. Most children have already given up, but six is different. She's smart, and tough, and has a lovely yellow raincoat. She belongs elsewhere.
The Twin Chef
A Pair of Brutes
With a love of violance and a feeling of meat, the twins were be born to be chefs. As they shuffle across the cold floor, chopping, mincing, preparing the feast, The Twin Chefs sense something that makes their skin itch. A dirty, unwelcome presence. Vermin will not be tolerated in the kitchen.
The Lady
A Watchfull eye
With Gracefull restraint, The Lady cast the hypnotic spell that keeps the engine running. Amidst the chaos of the world outside, the maw is the only place that makes sense, and now this rumour of an escaped child threatens evertything. Nothing can be allowed to interfere.
The Guest
Full to Bursting
The Guest are on essential part of the maw's life cycle. Herded into the large hall, they slump into their bowing stools, and there they feed. Without taste, without need, without end.
The Janitor
The Man with the Long Arms
with long forgotten things from long forgotten places, he fled the world and found The Maw. Now as the Janitor he is a tall tale hiding in the shadows, stalking the silence, a monster alone.
The Leeches
Bloodsuckers
In the dark, moist corner of the maw, the leeches writhe as one, awaiting the swollen wanderers. Slimy, venomous parasites that feed on the living.
Nomes
Playful Addities
Unpredictability can be find throughout The Maw, and so can the Nomes. Though experience has taught them to be skittish, some still try to make friends with strangers. These Nomes rarely survives.
